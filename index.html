Our Core Beliefs.

Anti-racism.

We stand against bigotry and racism irrespective of what form it takes against the Muslim community and other communities alike

Anti-Imperialism.

We stand with all Muslim and non-Muslim victims of the imperialist policies of the USFG. Imperialism has been the driving force behind the hawkish foreign policies against Muslim countries. Islamophobia has been a tool used to justify US foreign interests taking precedence over the lives many different Muslim communities.

Anti-Islamophobia.

We also stand against explicit and implicit forms of using Islam as the basis for discrimination. Whether Islamophobia explicitly motivates a Muslim ban in the US or implicitly justifies the use of drone strikes near civilian populations in the Middle East, we categorically call out all injustice stemming from Islamophobia

Who Are We.

In light of the recent challenges facing the Muslim community, we wanted to create a platform that can serve as a valuable tool for activists standing up the for the dignity of all Muslims. President Trump has become the more visible face of Islamophobia with his recent policies regarding the Muslim Ban and his continued promise to profile Muslims. Many families remain in fear and uncertain with how these policies are going impact their livelihood. These policies are among many other racist policies affecting our brothers and sisters in the Latinx, Black, LGBT, and minority resistance communities. We wanted to call for lifting and repealing all of these current and future policies in addition to pushing back on the Muslim Ban. We stand in solidarity with all families affected by the onslaught of these dehumanizing policies.

However, our stance goes beyond the visible face of Islamophobia apparent in President Trump and various communities in the US. We also want to call attention to the decade long state violence against Muslim communities across the world. The hawkish and opportunistic foreign policies in the Middle East has created many of the insurgencies and ‘fires’ that we are now dealing with.. More problematically, the disruption caused by our military occupation in the Middle East has created a vicious cycle of violence and instability. Whether it has been drone strikes that have killed several innocent civilians, or proxy wars that have propped up the terrorist organizations we are now fighting, Muslims have been the biggest victims of this violence from both sides for the past two decades.

We want to set the record straight. Our government’s aggressive foreign policies create the refugees that it wants to ban from entering into the US. Our resistance needs to address two fronts. We want to offer asylum and aid to refugees escaping violence. Equally if not more important, we want to call out the foreign policy that creates refugees to begin with. It's no coincidence that every country listed in the Muslim Ban has been the target of our occupation and drone strikes.

Ultimately, we want to maintain the dignity of the Muslims living in the US and ensure that refugees continue to have a welcome place in our communities. We also aim to create a world where there are no refugees and violence to escape from. When we say #TheMuslimResistance, we validate the resistance against the racist and imperialist forces that have victimized Muslims for far too long.

Our Demands.

Demand #1

We demand the unconditional repeal of the January 27, 2017 executive order preventing the acceptance of refugees from seven Muslim majority countries

Demand #2

We demand the removal of current and future programs to profile, track, and monitor Americans Muslims living in this country and in transit through this country

Demand #3

We demand the unconditional termination of the US military use of drone strikes

Demand #4

We demand the demilitarization and removal of occupation forces stationed in the Middle East

Demand #5

We demand the termination of all covert operations and affairs occurring throughout the Middle East

Demand #6

We demand the termination all programs that sanction the use deadly force, torture, or any form of coercion on Muslim communities worldwide
